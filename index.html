Part 1: Fundamentals and Scripture

This world is inverted. People are so programmed from birth that most ignore the Bible fundamentals. The devil lies and runs the kingdoms of earth. Jesus is Truth. Ask, Seek, Knock — God is the revealer of secrets (Daniel 2:22).

Understand the difference between corruptible seed and incorruptible (1 Peter 1:23), and the hybrid nature of our being due to two spiritual seedlines from Genesis 3:15.

The distinction between Elohim in Genesis 1 and the LORD God in Genesis 2 is crucial. Elohim is plural — gods, angels, magistrates — while the LORD God (YHWH Elohim) breathes the soul into Adam (Genesis 2:7). Egypt represents the world, a shadow of bondage we are spiritually led out from (Exodus 20:2).

Our birth into this world is a spiritual fall. We are angels in host bodies, entangled in a system run by Satan — the ruler of the kingdoms of the world (Matthew 4:8–9).

To be born again is to see this truth, to be converted, to turn back to the Father above (John 3:3, Matthew 18:3).
